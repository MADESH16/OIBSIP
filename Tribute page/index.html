MS DHONI Captain Cool Dhoni made his ODI debut on 23 December 2004, against Bangladesh in Chittagong, and played his first Test a year later against Sri Lanka. He played his first T20I also a year later against South Africa. His journey from a young cricketer making his ODI debut at the age of 23 to becoming one of the most successful captains in the history of Indian cricket is nothing short of remarkable. His calm and composed demeanor, both on and off the field, has earned him the nickname “Captain Cool”. Dhoni is the first cricketer to be a winning captain of all three major ICC trophies, as under his captaincy, India won the 2007 ICC World Twenty20, the 2011 World Cup, and the 2013 ICC Champions Trophy, the most by any captain. He also led India to victory in the 2010 and 2016 Asia Cup. Dhoni has been associated with the Chennai Super Kings (CSK) in the Indian Premier League (IPL) since the inaugural season in 2008. Under his captaincy, CSK has been one of the most successful franchises in the IPL, winning multiple championships.
